All TadaBot's Commands

/start - To start a giveaway
/edit - To edit the Prize of a specific giveaway
/delete - To delete a specific giveaway
/end - To end a specific giveaway
/reroll - To choose another Winner(s)

/help - To view all tadabot's commands
/ping - To see the bot's ping and you're ping
